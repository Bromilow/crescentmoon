Crawl Space Insulation

Protect your home from moisture, heat loss, and poor air quality with professionally installed crawl space insulation designed for Minnesota conditions.

Foundation Protection

Control Moisture. Improve Comfort.

The crawl space beneath your home plays a major role in indoor comfort, energy efficiency, and air quality. When a crawl space is poorly insulated or exposed to moisture, it allows cold air, dampness, and pollutants to move upward into living areas.

Crescent Moon Insulation provides crawl space insulation solutions that help stabilize indoor temperatures, protect structural components, and reduce moisture-related issues. Our approach focuses on sealing and insulating crawl spaces properly so your home performs efficiently year-round.

Whether your crawl space is vented, unvented, or partially conditioned, we assess the space and install insulation systems suited to your home's layout and environment.

Moisture Control

Insulation helps block ground moisture and reduces the risk of mold and mildew.

Energy Efficiency

Limiting air loss through the crawl space lowers heating and cooling demand.

Floor Comfort

Proper insulation helps keep floors warmer during cold months.

Structural Protection

Reducing moisture exposure helps protect framing and foundation materials.

Minnesota Homes

Why Crawl Space Insulation Matters

In Minnesota's climate, crawl spaces are especially vulnerable to cold temperatures and ground moisture. Without proper insulation, cold air can enter the home through floors and framing, increasing heating costs and causing uneven indoor temperatures.

Proper crawl space insulation helps reduce heat loss, limit moisture intrusion, and support healthier indoor air by minimizing drafts and condensation. Insulating this area is a key step in improving overall home efficiency and comfort.
