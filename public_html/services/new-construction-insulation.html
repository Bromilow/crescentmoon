New Construction Insulation

Get it right from the start. Expert insulation installation for new residential and commercial builds throughout Minnesota.

New Construction Specialist

Full Envelope Access. Done Right the First Time.

New construction is the best opportunity to get your insulation right. Before the drywall goes up, we have full access to the building envelope, walls, ceilings, floors, and everything in between. Crescent Moon Insulation works with builders, general contractors, and homeowners to install the ideal insulation system for every new build.

We help you meet Minnesota energy codes, maximize long-term efficiency, and ensure comfort from day one. We coordinate directly with your project timeline, showing up when needed and delivering results that make your build inspection-ready.

Full-Envelope Access

Installing insulation during construction means we can reach every cavity, header, and rim joist without compromise or costly retrofits.

Code-Ready Installation

We ensure your project meets or exceeds Minnesota Residential and Commercial Energy Code requirements.

Builder-Friendly Scheduling

We coordinate directly with your project timeline, showing up when needed and staying on schedule.

System Recommendations

Based on your plans and budget, we recommend the best insulation type for each area of the build, spray foam, blown-in, batt, or a hybrid approach.

Long-Term Performance

Insulation installed correctly during construction performs better and lasts longer than retrofit solutions, saving money for decades.

Start Right

Minnesota Homes Demand Expert Insulation from Day One

Minnesota's energy code exists for a reason: our climate is demanding, and buildings that are not properly insulated from the start will underperform for their entire lifespan. Retrofitting insulation later is always more expensive and less effective than doing it right during construction.

We work with builders across central Minnesota to ensure every new home and commercial building starts with a fully sealed, properly insulated envelope. The result is a structure that stays warm in winter, cool in summer, and energy-efficient year after year, exactly what Minnesota demands.

01

Plan Review

We review your architectural plans and identify the optimal insulation system for each area of the build, including walls, ceilings, basement, and crawl space.

02

Schedule Coordination

We coordinate with your project manager or general contractor to slot into the build schedule at the right phase, after rough framing and mechanical rough-in.

03

Installation

Our crew completes the full insulation scope efficiently and precisely, meeting depth, density, and coverage requirements for each area of the build.

04

Inspection Ready

We provide documentation for your building inspector and remain available for any follow-up questions or adjustments needed to close out the inspection.
