Commercial Expertise

Large-Scale Projects, Same Standard of Quality

Commercial buildings have unique insulation demands, larger scale, tighter timelines, and strict code requirements. Crescent Moon Insulation brings the same quality and attention to detail we are known for in residential work to every commercial project.

Whether you are building new, retrofitting an older structure, or upgrading to meet current energy standards, we deliver insulation solutions that reduce operating costs and improve occupant comfort. Minnesota Commercial Buildings

Operating Costs Start With Insulation

Operating a commercial building in Minnesota means contending with some of the harshest heating demands in the country. A poorly insulated warehouse, office, or retail space bleeds energy all winter long, and those costs add up fast at commercial scale. Proper insulation is not just a comfort upgrade; it is a direct investment in your operating efficiency.

We work with building owners, property managers, and general contractors across the state to ensure every commercial space is insulated to perform in our climate, meet current code, and deliver long-term energy savings. From pre-construction coordination to retrofit upgrades, we have the experience and equipment to handle commercial projects of any scope.

01

Project Review

We review building plans or conduct a site assessment to understand your insulation requirements, code obligations, and timeline.

02

Specification & Proposal

You receive a detailed written proposal covering insulation type, R-value specifications, scope, schedule, and pricing.

03

Coordinated Installation

Our crew works within your project timeline, coordinating with GCs and other trades to complete installation without causing delays.

04

We conduct a thorough post-installation inspection, provide documentation for permits if required, and confirm everything meets your project specs.
