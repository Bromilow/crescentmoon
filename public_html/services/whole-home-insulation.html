Insulation Solutions

Complete Insulation for Every Part of Your Home

Whole home insulation focuses on identifying and correcting energy loss throughout your entire property, not just one area. From attics and wall cavities to crawl spaces and basements, every section of your home plays a role in comfort and efficiency.

Crescent Moon Insulation provides comprehensive insulation solutions that address the full building envelope. We assess existing insulation, identify air leaks, and recommend the right insulation types for each area to ensure consistent temperatures and improved indoor comfort year-round.

Rather than guessing which insulation upgrade will make the biggest impact, our team develops a clear plan that improves performance across the entire home.

Minnesota Homes

Energy Efficiency Works Best as a System

In Minnesota's climate, insulating only one part of a home often leaves other areas working against it. Heat loss through walls, floors, attics, and crawl spaces can add up quickly, forcing heating and cooling systems to work harder than necessary.

Whole home insulation improves energy efficiency by treating the home as a connected system. By sealing air leaks and upgrading insulation where it matters most, homeowners can reduce energy costs, eliminate drafts, and maintain more stable indoor temperatures in every season.
