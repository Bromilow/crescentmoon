Air Duct Sealing and Cleaning

Improve indoor air quality, HVAC efficiency, and energy performance with professional air duct sealing and cleaning across Minnesota.

System Performance Solutions

Cleaner Air Starts in Your Ductwork

Air ducts play a critical role in distributing heated and cooled air throughout your home or building. Over time, dust, debris, and leaks in the duct system can reduce HVAC efficiency, increase energy costs, and negatively impact indoor air quality.

Crescent Moon Insulation provides professional air duct sealing and cleaning services to restore system performance. By removing contaminants and sealing air leaks, we help ensure air reaches living and working spaces efficiently while reducing strain on heating and cooling equipment.

Improved Air Quality

Reduces dust, allergens, and airborne contaminants.

Increased HVAC Efficiency

Sealed ducts deliver conditioned air where it’s needed.

Consistent Temperatures

Eliminates hot and cold spots throughout the space.

Extended System Life

Reduces strain on heating and cooling equipment.

Minnesota Homes & Buildings

Energy Loss Often Hides in the Ducts

Leaky or dirty ductwork forces HVAC systems to work harder to maintain indoor comfort. Air escaping through gaps, tears, or poorly sealed connections leads to higher utility bills, uneven temperatures, and unnecessary system wear.

Professional air duct sealing reduces energy loss, while thorough cleaning removes dust, allergens, and contaminants that circulate through indoor air. Together, these services improve comfort, efficiency, and long-term system reliability.
