Insulation Removal Services

Safe, professional removal of damaged, contaminated, or outdated insulation to restore energy efficiency and indoor air quality.

Professional Insulation Removal

Out With the Old. Done the Right Way.

When insulation becomes old, damaged, or contaminated, it can no longer protect your home or building effectively. Water damage, pest infestations, mold growth, and general degradation all reduce insulation performance and can negatively impact indoor air quality and energy efficiency.

Crescent Moon Insulation provides professional insulation removal services for attics, crawl spaces, and other areas. Our trained team uses proper equipment and safety protocols to remove compromised insulation cleanly and efficiently, preparing your space for modern, high-performance insulation.

Improved Energy Efficiency

Eliminates ineffective insulation that causes heat loss and energy waste.

Better Indoor Air Quality

Removes contaminants, allergens, odors, and mold-affected materials.

Pest & Moisture Control

Addresses insulation compromised by rodents, insects, or water damage.

Prep for New Insulation

Creates a clean foundation for modern, high-performance materials.

Minnesota Homes & Buildings

Damaged Insulation Costs You More

Old or contaminated insulation can lead to higher energy bills, uneven indoor temperatures, poor air quality, and long-term structural concerns. Moisture-damaged insulation can promote mold growth, while rodent-infested insulation poses health risks and odors.

Removing failing insulation is a critical first step toward restoring comfort, safety, and efficiency. Once removed, your building is ready for upgraded insulation solutions designed for Minnesota's climate.
