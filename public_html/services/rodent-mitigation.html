Rodent Mitigation Services

Professional rodent control and prevention solutions for residential and commercial properties across Minnesota.

Property Protection

Stop Rodent Damage at the Source

Rodent infestations pose serious risks to both property and health. From damaged insulation and wiring to contamination from droppings and urine, rodents can quickly compromise the safety and comfort of a home or commercial building.

Crescent Moon Insulation provides professional rodent mitigation services designed to eliminate active infestations and prevent future entry. Our approach focuses on identifying access points, removing existing rodents, and securing vulnerable areas to protect your building long term.

Entry Point Sealing

We identify and secure gaps, vents, and access points rodents use to enter buildings.

Active Rodent Removal

Safe and effective removal methods tailored to the property and infestation level.

Insulation Protection

Rodent-damaged insulation is identified and addressed to restore performance.

Long-Term Prevention

Solutions designed to reduce the risk of rodents returning.

Minnesota Rodent Control

Prevention Is More Effective Than Repair

Rodents such as mice and rats can cause extensive structural damage, contaminate insulation, and spread disease if left unaddressed. Effective rodent mitigation goes beyond trapping and removal. It requires sealing entry points, addressing nesting areas, and reducing conditions that attract rodents to the property.

Our rodent mitigation services are designed to protect insulation systems, improve indoor air quality, and reduce the risk of costly repairs caused by ongoing rodent activity.

01

Property Inspection

We assess rodent activity, entry points, and affected areas.

02

Mitigation Plan

A targeted strategy is developed to remove rodents and secure the structure.

03

Implementation

Rodent removal, sealing, and preventative measures are completed.

04

Final Review

We confirm mitigation effectiveness and recommend ongoing prevention steps.
