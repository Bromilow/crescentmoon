Minnesota Sound Mitigation

Reducing Noise Starts With the Building Envelope

Sound mitigation works by absorbing, blocking, and dampening sound energy as it moves through a structure. Using a combination of insulation upgrades and sound-dampening materials, we help reduce airborne noise and vibration transfer through walls, ceilings, and floors.

Our approach is tailored to each property, whether the goal is reducing exterior noise, improving privacy between rooms, or enhancing acoustics in spaces such as offices, home theaters, or studios. While no system can eliminate all noise, proper materials and installation can significantly reduce sound levels and improve overall comfort.
