Wall Insulation Services

Reduce heat loss, improve comfort, and increase energy efficiency with professionally installed wall insulation.

Insulation Expertise

Protecting Comfort from the Inside Out

Poorly insulated walls allow heat to escape during winter and let cooled air leak out during summer, putting constant strain on your heating and cooling systems. Wall insulation plays a critical role in maintaining consistent indoor temperatures and improving overall energy efficiency.

Crescent Moon Insulation works with homeowners to assess existing wall insulation levels and identify areas where heat loss, drafts, or noise transfer may be occurring. Whether insulating interior walls, exterior walls, or upgrading older homes, we deliver solutions designed to improve comfort and reduce energy waste.

Minnesota Homes

Energy Efficiency Starts in the Walls

Many older homes were built with little or no wall insulation, resulting in uneven temperatures, drafts, and higher utility bills. Exterior walls are especially vulnerable, as they form a primary barrier between your living space and outdoor conditions.

Proper wall insulation helps reduce heat transfer, limits noise between rooms, and improves year-round comfort. Whether insulation is added during siding replacement or retrofitted using modern methods, upgrading wall insulation delivers measurable performance improvements.
